Mrs. A's Custom Cheer Bows is a non-profit retail business specializing in B2C commerce. We generally work with customers on an individual basis, but occasionally fill a bulk or mass order for customers who provide validation of team status to ensure that our product is not used for re-sale. We produce hand-made, customized cheer-style bows for use as a hair accessory or decoration, and each bow is made to the specifications of the individual buyer.
