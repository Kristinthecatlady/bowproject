Target Market

Our target is girls ages 13-18 who are involved and/or interested in a local high school or all-star cheer program. Our target is fashion-conscious, and likes to do something special with her hair almost every day. She lives at home with a parent in the Pacific Northwest, where she was raised with an appreciation for supporting small, local businesses. Her family buys groceries at local farmers markets, and once a month the volunteer at the local animal shelter. She often babysits her cousin for extra spending money, and always makes sure her grades are on track so that she can continue cheering. She and her friends love to get together and paint their nails, trade clothes, and practice their routines.
